How it works

Three quick steps from photo to guidance.

1

Upload a close-up

Drop or paste a well-lit, in-focus photo of the lesion. We first check it actually looks like skin.

2

AI screening

A CNN trained on HAM10000 estimates probabilities across 7 lesion types and a cancer-risk score.

3

Export & act

Get plain-language next steps and download a shareable report for your dermatologist.

What's inside

Built to be useful, honest, and easy to share.

📊

Full breakdown

📄

Exportable advice

Context aware

Fast & private
